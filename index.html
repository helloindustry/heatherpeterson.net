Heather Peterson is a passionate and accomplished classical pianist and private teacher who masterfully blends the highest level of technique with fundamental humanity.

Teaching from her studio in Oak Park, Illinois, she delights in helping each student access, develop, and release his or her unique voice into musical expression.

Heather holds a master's degree in piano performance from the University of Cincinnati College-Conservatory of Music, and a bachelor's degree in piano from Northern Illinois University. She has been teaching private piano lessons from her own studio for over 25 years and has held music, piano and choral teaching positions at Illinois Central College, Peoria Academy, Aletheia Classical Academy, and professional accompanying positions at Opera Illinois, and Morton Community Chorus, and Oak Park and River Forest High School.

 
 

Heather began giving full-length solo recitals at the age of eight, and has placed as finalist or winner of many regional, national and international piano competitions. She has performed publicly as a soloist and as a competition winner with several orchestras.

Heather lives in Oak Park with her husband, and four amazing musical children.
